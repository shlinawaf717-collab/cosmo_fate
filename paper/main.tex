\begin{abstract}
% v1 numbers locked except F_param sentence — see OUTLINE.md
The DESI era has produced a wave of claims about the ultimate fate of the
Universe. We ask a different question: how much of the fate verdict is forced
by the data, and how much is donated by the assumptions? Under a preregistered
analysis plan (frozen before any real-data fate analysis; two logged
amendments), we combine DESI DR2 BAO, \textit{Planck} 2018 distance priors,
and Pantheon+/SH0ES supernovae under the CPL parametrization, and propagate
every posterior sample to $a=10^4$ through a frozen fate classifier.

\end{abstract}
